What is Florocassion?
Here at FlorOccasion, we are dedicated to making the best quality flower boxes, guaranteed to brighten up anyone’s day. No matter the occasion, our handmade, bespoke flower boxes are the perfect gift to lift someone’s spirit and put a smile on their face!

Our Flower arrangements are created to suit your specific requests. We have a wide range of flower box designs and sizes which can be made with soap or fresh roses.

We are a small team that specialises in the creation of handmade flower boxes for all kinds of ocassions!

Our
lightly scented soap roses
come in a variety of beautiful colours and are ideal to be used as long-lasting ornamental flower displays, providing a fresh scent and an elegant, sophisticated look to your home. Our soap roses also dissolve in warm water and can be used in the bath.
Our
fresh rose boxes
are a unique alternative to traditional bouquets, combining our modern luxury boxes with classic roses, a choice for those who prefer a more natural option.
